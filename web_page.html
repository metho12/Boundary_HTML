Alzheimer’s disease

  • Alzheimer’s disease, or AD, is a widely increasing disorder of the brain that is affecting many people worldwide.
  • It is regarded as the main cause of memory loss and dementia. Early diagnosis of AD is an important factor that helps make plans for AD patients and prevent the development of symptoms of this disease.
  • There are various non-invasive techniques that can be used for an early diagnosis of the disease, such as an electroencephalogram (EEG), func- tional magnetic resonance imaging (fMRI), diffusion tensor imaging (DTI), and positron emission tomography (PET) neuroimaging
